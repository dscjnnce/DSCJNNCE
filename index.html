What is Developers Student Club?


DSC JNNCE is a group of student developers with a same motive of make life easier
and by bringing new innovative ideas and to make others understand. We are more focused on Web development, Web designing, Graphic designing, Android app development,
UI/UX designing, Research and Communication. Here, in DSC JNNCE we believe in learning
and grow together so that we can teach other students what we have learnt so far. So that they can be trainers in the future.
