What is Developers Student Club?


DSC JNNCE is a group of student developers with a same motive of make life easier
and by bringing new innovative ideas and to make others understand. We are more focused on
Web development, Web designing, Graphic designing, Android app development,
UI/UX designing, Research and Communication. Here, in DSC JNNCE we believe in learn
and grow together so we teach other students what we have learnt so far. So that they can be trainers.
